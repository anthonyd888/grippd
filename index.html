About Gripped!

Gripped is a small company founded by skaters and designers that wanted a different type of grip tape. We became bored of the name brand grip tapes we saw everyone riding and decided to make our own designs into rideable grip! After successfully making our own, we decided to share our designs with the rest of the world!
